Senior Capstone Project

Project Overview

For my senior capstone project, I worked on a semester long project and competition to redesign the system, design, and functionality of the client's website "Madison Connect Hub", established by our client, Chris O'Brien. Madison Connect Hub acts as a hub that allows both students and faculties to connect and form a team as well as combining passions and skills to start a project. My group - "Hartman Consultants" was made up of two students from Media Arts and Design and six students from College of Business to design a completely new information system. In the span of two months, we completed three sprints and a final presentation to deliver the product to our client.


The task given to our team was to replace the database, UX/UI design, and information system all together from scratch by teaming up with the computer information system students. Based on the first meeting with our client, we understood that Madison Connect Hub was in need of a better UI/visual hierarchy and functionality so that this website can not only help users connect with others, but to also give users a reason to return and use the website.

User Research

For my role in the team, I was responsible for conducting interviews, data analysis, and creating the wireframes and prototypes. Since the website could only be used for JMU students and faculties, I conducted interviews and usability testing with two JMU students and faculties to gain further insights into the previous version of the website. From there, we figured out the needs, wants, and painpoints of the website, which we summarized it into "user stories" and "personas."

User Stories


01 - As a user, I want an improved visual hierarchy of the website and placements of icons and buttons, so I can navigate the website with ease.

02 - As a user, I want multiple search bars, so I can easily search for other students, faculty, or projects.

03- As a user, I want several message options besides email, so I can easily reach out to collaborators.

04- As a user, I want to able to bookmark/friend other users, so I can accumulate a list of people I liked to work with or people I want to work with.

05- As a user, I want a chat feature on the website to easily communicate and update the progress of the project.
